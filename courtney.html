Courtney Higgins

(Charlotte and surrounding areas)


About

Meet Courtney, the lead trainer of our North & South Carolina locations. Courtney has always been an animal lover, working in dog kennels, vet clinics, and at horse farms all her life. Her training career sparked when she turned 18 and rescued a dog and people aggressive pit bull boxer mix named Bella. She was the head pet trainer at PetSmart for 5 years, which gave her a great head start into the training world. She got hired on with Off Leash Dog Training and worked there for 5 years where she learned how to rehab people and dog aggressive dogs. She now has decided to work with us at Steadfast and help us expand our training resources to the Carolinas. You can always find Courtney with her husband Kent, and her son Mason, at the barn with her horse; always with a dog by her side.
