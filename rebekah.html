Rebekah Farmer

(Tampa and surrounding areas)


About

Meet Rebekah, owner of Steadfast Dog Training and Lead trainer of our Tampa location! Rebekah is an Animal Behavior College certified dog trainer as well as an AKC Canine Good Citizen Evaluator (Evaluator #91094). Although, she has always been a dog lover her formerly aggressive dog Lola is what sparked her interest in a career of dog training. She has years of training experience and has worked with a vast amount of breeds and behavioral issues! She loves to hang out with her husband (AKA Uncle Mitch), their dogs, and training with dogs outdoors! Rebekah, Uncle Mitch and their pack of four dogs love to hike and take trips to the dog beach!
